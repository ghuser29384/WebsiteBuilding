Volunteer and Judge Recruitment

CHSEB is hiring volunteers to run tournaments and judges with strong backgrounds in philosophy, ethics, or related disciplines.

Open Roles

Tournament Operations Volunteer

Support scheduling, room moderation, team check-in, scoring logistics, and communications.

Philosophy Judge

Evaluate rounds with explicit attention to argument quality, conceptual clarity, and ethical depth.

Coach and Training Volunteer

Help onboard new schools, run mock rounds, and train students in constructive dialogue norms.

Preferred Judge Profile (Philosophy Background)

  • Background in philosophy, ethics, political theory, law, or closely related fields.
  • Capacity to evaluate argument structure rather than rhetorical speed.
  • Comfort engaging plural perspectives without lowering analytic rigor.
  • Bilingual fluency (English and Mandarin) is strongly welcomed but not mandatory.

What Volunteers Help Deliver

Pre-Event Setup

Calendar coordination, judge calibration calls, and technical checks.

Tournament Day

Session moderation, scoring workflow support, and participant assistance.

Post-Event Follow Up

Feedback summaries, educational debriefs, and regional growth planning.
