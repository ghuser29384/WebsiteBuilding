24-Hour Online Meeting Rooms

Join open rooms for continuous philosophical discussion. Each room has a thematic focus and starter prompts.

Room Guidelines

  • Respect speaking turns and summarize before rebutting.
  • Use claims, reasons, and counterexamples; avoid personal attacks.
  • Welcome bilingual participation and clarify key terms when needed.
  • If room traffic is high, create breakout groups and report back.
