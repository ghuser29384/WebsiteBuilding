Registration and Contact

Register school teams, join the judge pool, or contact organizers for implementation support.

Participation Pathways

Student Teams

Schools may register one or more 3-5 student teams with a coach advisor.

Judges & Moderators

Teachers, graduate students, and trained volunteers can apply as judges or moderators.

Regional Hosts

Schools and education groups can host regional rounds with CHSEB support materials.

Registration Steps

  1. Download the bilingual rulebook and casebook from Resources.
  2. Prepare school/team information and a coach contact person.
  3. Submit inquiry email with your intended participation pathway.
  4. CHSEB staff reply with confirmation timeline and onboarding packet.
